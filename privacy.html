This Privacy Policy describes howVasuvo Care ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Vasuvo Care mobile application ("App").

Vasuvo Care is a consumer wellness and caregiver support application. It is not a medical provider or healthcare service.

This Policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and applies to users in India and internationally.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to provide our services.

A. Account Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • State / Location (if voluntarily provided)

B. Caregiver & Patient Information

  • Linked caregiver details
  • Relationship type
  • Emergency contact details

C. Health & Reminder Information

  • Medication names
  • Dosage details
  • Reminder schedules
  • Health notes entered by user
  • Reminder completion logs
  • Activity / inactivity check data (if enabled)
⚠️ We do NOT collect medical records, prescriptions, insurance information, or hospital data.

D. Location Information

  • Precise device location (GPS) (only when you grant location permission; used for caregiver features and fraud prevention)

E. In-App Messages & Communications

  • In-app messages, notifications, and caregiver-to-member communications sent within the app

F. Files & Documents

  • Files or documents you choose to upload or share within the app (e.g. prescription images, health reports) (optional)

G. App Activity & Interactions

  • App interactions (screens visited, features used, buttons tapped)
  • Reminder completion and skip events
  • Session duration and frequency of use

H. Device & Technical Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version
  • Device or advertising ID (device or other IDs)
  • Push notification token
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • Network connection type

We use your information to:

  • Enable caregiver notifications

4. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside India (including the United States), your information may be transferred to and processed in India where our servers or service providers are located.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We implement reasonable safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data:

  • Encrypted in transit: All data transferred between your device and our servers uses HTTPS / TLS encryption
  • Encrypted at rest: Stored data is encrypted on our secure cloud infrastructure
  • Role-based access control — only authorised personnel can access personal data
  • Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security monitoring and vulnerability assessments
🔒 Data is encrypted in transit — your data is transferred over a secure connection at all times.

No digital system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@vasuvo.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • Until you request deletion
  • As required under applicable law

Upon account deletion, personal data will be permanently erased within 30 days of your confirmed deletion request, unless legally required to retain specific records (e.g. transaction records are kept for 7 years under financial regulations; support correspondence for 3 years for fraud prevention). Retained data is never used for marketing.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals below 18 years of age without guardian supervision.

If we learn that personal data has been collected without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.

10. Account Deletion

You have the right to request permanent deletion of your Vasuvo Care account and all associated personal data at any time. You may submit a deletion request via any of the following methods:

  • Online form (recommended): vasuvo.com/delete-account — submit your request with your registered email address and preferred deletion scope
  • In-app: App Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: contact@vasuvo.com with subject line “Account Deletion Request”

You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours. Your account and personal data will be permanently erased within 30 days of that confirmation. See Section 7 for details on what data is retained for legal compliance.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All medication schedules, health logs, and family profiles will be permanently removed.
