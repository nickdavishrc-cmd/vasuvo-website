## 1. Getting Started

### Who is Vasuvo Care for?
Vasuvo Care has two user types — you choose when you register:

| Role | Who | What they do |
|---|---|---|
| **Member / Patient** | The person taking medication | Manages their own medications, logs vitals, gets reminders |
| **Care Partner** | Family member, son/daughter, spouse | Monitors the patient, gets missed-dose alerts, views records |

### Step 1 — Download & Open
Install Vasuvo Care from the Google Play Store and open it.

### Step 2 — Choose Your Role
On the **Role Selection** screen:
- Tap **"I am a Member or Patient"** if you are the one taking medicine
- Tap **"I am a Care Partner"** if you are looking after someone else

### Step 3 — Register
1. Enter your **full name** and **mobile number**
2. An **OTP** will be sent — enter it to verify your number
3. Create a **4–6 digit PIN** (used for daily login — no password to remember)
4. Read and accept the **Medical Disclaimer**

### Step 4 — Log In
Open the app → enter your phone number → enter your PIN → you're in.

## 2. Home Screen

The Home Screen is your daily health dashboard. It shows:

- **Today's Medications** — every dose due today with status (Pending / Taken / Missed)
- **Medication Streak** — your consecutive days of perfect adherence (motivational tracker)
- **Allergy Alert** — a red banner if you have recorded allergies (always visible for safety)
- **Quick Actions** — fast access to Location Sharing, Medical Records, and more
- **Member View** (Care Partners only) — switch between connected patients using the member selector at the top

### Marking a Dose as Taken
On the Home Screen, find the medication card and tap **"Mark as Taken"**. The status updates immediately and your streak is preserved.

## 3. Medications

### Adding a Medication
1. Go to **Home → Medications** (or tap the pill icon in the bottom nav)
2. Tap the **➕ Add Medication** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — start typing; the app suggests real drug names from a clinical database
   - **Type** — Tablet, Capsule, Liquid, Injection, Syrup, Gel, Patch, Inhaler, Drop, or Other
   - **Dosage** — tap a suggestion chip (e.g. "500 mg") or type your own
   - **Frequency** — how many times per day (1×, 2×, 3× etc.)
   - **Times** — pick exact reminder times for each dose
   - **Days** — choose specific days or "Every Day"
   - **Start Date** — when the course begins
   - **Supply** — optionally track how many pills you have left
4. Tap **Save** — reminders are scheduled immediately

### Editing or Deleting a Medication
Open **Medications** → tap any medication → tap the **edit** (pencil) or **delete** (trash) icon.

### Medication Reminders
- Reminders fire as **push notifications** at the exact times you set
- Tap the notification to open the app and mark the dose as taken
- If you miss a dose, a **missed-dose escalation** runs automatically:
  - **Level 1** — reminder resent to you
  - **Level 2** — your Care Partner is notified
  - **Level 3** — SMS alert sent (if configured)

### Supply Tracking
If you enter a supply count when adding a medication, the app counts down with each dose. When supply is running low, you'll get a refill reminder.

## 4. Health Tracker (Vitals)

Track your key health numbers over time and see them on charts.

### Supported Vital Types
| Vital | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Blood Pressure | Systolic / Diastolic (e.g. 120/80) |
| Blood Sugar | mg/dL or mmol/L |
| Weight | kg or lbs |
| Heart Rate | beats per minute |
| Temperature | °C or °F |
| Oxygen Saturation | SpO₂ % |
| Cholesterol (LDL / HDL) | mg/dL |
| HbA1c | % |
| Waist Circumference | cm |
| Triglycerides | mg/dL |

### Logging a Reading
1. Open **Health Tracker** (bottom nav or Home quick action)
2. Tap the **➕** next to any vital type
3. Enter the value and confirm
4. The reading is saved and the chart updates

### Viewing Charts
Tap any vital card to expand a line chart showing your history. Trend direction (improving / worsening) is highlighted.

### Care Partner View
Care Partners can switch to their connected Member's vitals using the member selector — read-only view of the patient's health data.

### Daily Vitals Reminders
The app sends you a reminder at **8 AM and 10 AM** via server-side push notification — no matter whether the app is open or closed, these come through reliably every morning.

## 5. AI Insights (AI Clinic)

Personalized health intelligence powered by Google Gemini AI — tailored specifically to **your medications and health conditions**.

### What AI Insights Shows
- **Drug Interaction Warnings** — flags dangerous combinations in your medication list
- **Diet Tips** — foods to eat more of based on your medications
- **Foods to Be Cautious Of** — what to avoid (e.g. grapefruit with certain statins)
- **Lifestyle Recommendations** — sleep, exercise, and daily habits
- **Hydration Tips** — water intake guidance based on your medication profile
- **Condition Detection** — automatically recognizes chronic condition patterns from your medication names

### How to Access
Tap **AI Clinic** in the bottom navigation or from the For You tab.

### Refreshing Insights
Tap the **refresh** icon (top right) to generate a fresh analysis. Insights are cached and auto-refresh periodically so you always see current recommendations.

### Care Plus — Custom Questions (AI Clinic Full Access)
Care Plus subscribers can type **any health question** and get an AI answer in addition to the automated insights.

## 15. Activity Monitoring (Care Plus)

**Care Plus exclusive.** Passive safety monitoring for Members (patients) — no action needed from them.

### How it Works
- The app detects when a Member has not been active for **4+ hours**
- Automatically sends a push notification to all connected Care Partners
- Alert levels escalate based on duration:
  - **MEDIUM** — 4 hours inactive
  - **HIGH** — 6 hours inactive
  - **EMERGENCY** — 8+ hours inactive

### Care Partner Alert
The notification reads:
> "⚠️ [Name] has not been active for X hours. Please check on them."

Care Partners can tap the notification to open the app and respond.

## 21. Notifications — How They Work

Vasuvo Care uses three types of notifications to ensure you never miss anything:

### 1. Medication Reminders
- Delivered by your device's alarm system at exact scheduled times
- Also backed by server-side push via Google FCM
- Tap the notification → app opens → mark dose as taken

### 2. Morning Vitals Reminders
- Sent at **8 AM and 10 AM** daily by the Vasuvo server via Google FCM
- Arrives even if the app is completely closed
- Not affected by battery optimization settings

### 3. Missed Dose & Safety Alerts
- Sent by the Vasuvo server when a dose is missed or inactivity is detected
- Delivered via Google FCM — bypasses battery optimization

### Notification Permissions
On first launch, the app will ask for notification permission. **Always tap Allow** — without it, medication reminders cannot be delivered.

## 23. Tips & Troubleshooting

### Notifications not coming?
1. Go to **Settings → Apps → Vasuvo Care → Notifications** → make sure all channels are enabled
2. Check that **"Pause app activity if unused"** is OFF (Settings → Apps → Vasuvo Care)
3. Vitals reminders come via Google's servers — they work even when the app is closed

### The app shows old data?
Pull down on any screen to **force refresh**. The app auto-refreshes every minute in the background.

### How do I switch between patients? (Care Partners)
On the Home screen, tap the **Member selector** at the top to switch between your connected Members.

### My adherence report is missing data?
The Member must grant **Adherence Report consent** in their Connection settings. Ask them to go to **Connections → [your name] → Consent → Adherence Reports → Enable**.

### I forgot my PIN?
Tap **"Forgot PIN?"** on the login screen → enter your registered phone number → an OTP is sent → reset your PIN.

### How do I cancel Care Plus?
Go to **Profile → Subscription → Switch to Free**. You retain Care Plus access until the end of the current billing period.
