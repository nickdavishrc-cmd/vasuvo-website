Delete Your Vasuvo Care Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. Submit the form below and our team will process your request promptly.

📋 How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Fill in the account deletion request form on this page with your registered email address and full name.
  2. Select the scope of deletion you want — full account deletion or data-only deletion.
  3. Confirm you understand that this action is irreversible by ticking the acknowledgement checkbox.
  4. Submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email at your registered address within 24 hours.
  5. Our team will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days of confirmation.
  6. You will receive a final email confirming that your account and data have been deleted.

🗂️ Data Deleted vs. Retained

Data Type Status Retention
Account profile (name, email, phone) Deleted 30 days after request
Family member profiles Deleted 30 days after request
Medication schedules & reminders Deleted 30 days after request
Adherence history & health logs Deleted 30 days after request
App usage & analytics data Deleted 30 days after request
Transaction / payment records Retained 7 years (legal obligation)
Aggregated anonymised statistics Retained Indefinite (not linked to you)
Support correspondence Retained 3 years (fraud prevention)

Retained data is kept solely to meet legal, tax, and fraud-prevention obligations. It will never be used for marketing.

📧 Prefer to Email Us?

Send your deletion request directly to contact@vasuvo.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.
