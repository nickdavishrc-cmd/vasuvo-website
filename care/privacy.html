This Privacy Policy describes how Vasuvo HealthTech Pvt Ltd ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Vasuvo mobile application ("App").

Vasuvo is a consumer wellness and caregiver support application. It is not a medical provider or healthcare service.

This Policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and applies to users in India and internationally.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to provide our services.

A. Account Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • State / Location (if voluntarily provided)

B. Caregiver & Patient Information

  • Linked caregiver details
  • Relationship type
  • Emergency contact details

C. Health & Reminder Information

  • Medication names
  • Dosage details
  • Reminder schedules
  • Health notes entered by user
  • Reminder completion logs
  • Activity / inactivity check data (if enabled)
⚠️ We do NOT collect medical records, prescriptions, insurance information, or hospital data.

D. Device & Technical Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Notification token
  • Crash logs
  • Basic analytics data

2. Lawful Basis for Processing (India – DPDP Act)

We process personal data based on:

  • User consent at registration
  • Performance of service (reminders & caregiver linking)
  • Legal compliance where required
ℹ️ You may withdraw consent at any time.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide medication reminders
  • Enable caregiver notifications
  • Detect inactivity (if enabled)
  • Send safety alerts
  • Improve app functionality
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain security and fraud prevention
🔒

We DO NOT sell personal data.

We DO NOT use your data for targeted advertising.

4. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside India (including the United States), your information may be transferred to and processed in India where our servers or service providers are located.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We implement reasonable safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

5. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with:

  • Secure cloud hosting providers
  • SMS or notification service providers
  • Analytics providers (non-health behavioral data only)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

All service providers are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality.

🚫 We do NOT sell, rent, or trade user data.

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS / TLS)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Role-based access control
  • Limited employee access
  • Regular security monitoring

No digital system is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • Until you request deletion
  • As required under applicable law

Upon account deletion, personal data will be permanently erased within [X] working days unless legally required to retain it.
