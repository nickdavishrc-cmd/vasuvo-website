Frequently asked questions

What is a medication adherence app?
A medication adherence app helps you organise complex medication regimens, set timely pill reminders, and track your daily doses. Vasuvo Care goes further — it also syncs caregivers, logs missed doses, and sends prescription refill alerts, so your whole family stays on track.
How is Vasuvo Care different from other medication reminder apps?
Most medication apps are built for a single user. Vasuvo Care is built for the whole family — with separate profiles for each member, caregiver syncing, and chronic disease management tools for conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease. It's the only health app designed around how families actually manage health together.
Can I use Vasuvo Care for an elderly parent?
Yes. Vasuvo Care is specifically designed with seniors in mind — large text, simple navigation, and calm reminders. Family members and caregivers can monitor adherence remotely, so you always know if your parent has taken their medication, even from a distance.
Does Vasuvo Care support chronic disease management?
Yes. Vasuvo Care includes dedicated support for people living with diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and other long-term conditions. This includes medication scheduling, adherence tracking, and reminders tailored to chronic condition regimens.
Is my health data safe with Vasuvo Care?
Absolutely. Vasuvo Care is GDPR compliant and all health data is encrypted end-to-end. Your data is yours — we never sell it or share it with third parties without your explicit consent.
